Gearlock stops characters from 'blinking' when they swap gear.

Commands
-All settings default to on.
-Commands with [on/off] will be used as a toggle if it is not specified.

/gl blink - Update all models to what they're actually wearing.
/gl self [on/off]   - When enabled, will stop you from blinking.
/gl target [on/off] - When enabled, will stop your target from blinking.
/gl others [on/off] - When enabled, will stop all other PCs from blinking.
/gl invis [on/off]  - When enabled, will make players with invisible status appear visible.
